Athlete Infographics - 2016 Summer Olympics

2016 Summer Olympic was held from 5 to 21 August 2016 in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil with more than 11,000 athletes from 207 nations participating in the multi-sport event. These includes first-time entrants from Kosovo, South Sudan, and the Refugee Olympic Team.
